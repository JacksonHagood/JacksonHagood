Villa Savoye II

In 2014 I started my first major modern creative world, Villa Savoye. In this world I was able to explore contemporary design in Minecraft and formulate my own identity. In 2016 I began work on its sequel, Villa Savoye II, which I am working on to this day. Villa Savoye II is my largest single effort in Minecraft, with over 500 hours in the world. Villa Savoye II now has hundreds of houses, a city, and many secrets.

In order to allow for unhindered creativity, I created my own resource pack by combining several others. The result is mostly composed of textures from Flows HD, UTB, and Cubed. I created the world with mods and tools like World Painter, World Edit, and Single Player Commands. I decided to stop updating the world after 1.12.2 as newer versions would present issues with the resource pack and performance (and because the world would not significantly benefit from any new features). The world is best viewed in Optifine with Sildurs Vibrant shaders and the resource pack is a requirement.

I have decided to not post the world for download until it is completed. Currently, I plan to call the world "complete" on the 10-year anniversary, though this might change. Honestly, I may never call the world complete.

Palette Island

My most regent major project in Villa Savoye II was Palette Island. The island consists of 12 houses, 9 skyscrapers, 3 farms, and several parks. The island is built around a large nature preserve in the center with all buildings along the coast. On side of the island consists of the majority of the houses in a single row. The opposite side is where the city is located. On the smaller ends there are farms and the rest of the houses. I am currently working on the neighboring island.
