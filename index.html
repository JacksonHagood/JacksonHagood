About

This May I graduated with a degree in Computer Engineering from Texas A&M University with a 4.0 GPA. I will forever treasure my time as a student and teaching assistant at Texas A&M. In the last four years I have developed a love and appreciation for computer science and mathematics that I hope to pass on to others. This August I will be rejoining JPMorgan Chase & Co. as a Software Engineer (SEP) in Plano, TX.

Most experience with C++, Java, and Python. I have developed front-end applications in JavaScript with React, Redux, and Material UI. Back-end experience in Spring Boot and Flask. Throughout my studies and personal projects I have explored programming language theory, with languages like Haskell, and low level programming in ARM assembly language. I have great interest in mathematics, including topics in number theory and cryptography.

This website serves as a place to host my qualifications and resume along with my projects and personal interests. Education is a life long endeavor and I seek to never stop learning and giving back to the world.
